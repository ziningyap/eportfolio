Instructions for Use

system for Trulicity

Comprising of an instruction sheet and an online guide, the Instructions for Use (IFU) system aims to offer a smooth-sailing and personal experience for the users of Trulicity, a self-injecting medication for type 2 diabetes.

The instruction sheet presents instructions strategically and has elements of human touch to make the information more approachable and less overwhelming. The online guide leverages on progressive disclosure to enable users to control how they want to receive their instructions through the interface. Moreover, instructions are animated with audio guide to better engage and comfort the users.

Key Words

Instructions, Information Visualization, UI/UX, Medical

Target Audience

New users of Trulicity who are nervous about administering their self-injections

Goals

  • Assist users in administering their injections with confidence and minimal hesitation
  • Help users easily understand how to properly handle their medicine

Year of project

2020
