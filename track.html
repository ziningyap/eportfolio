TRACK

DIY tracking device for occupational therapy

Tapping on a dependable means of tracking motion via computer vision, TRACK is an accessible and low-cost DIY tracking device for Occupational Therapy. It aims to assist the Occupational Therapists (OTs) in monitoring the elderly’s performance and progress while also improving the elderly’s rehabilitation experience.

Target Audience

Elderly and Occupational Therapists in Rehabilitation Centres

Goals

  • Engage and motivate elderly in completing their exercise by gamifying it
  • Help Occupational Therapists to make more accurate prescriptions by tracking the elderly’s performance and progress

Designed with Pang Shi Jie and Elicia Low

What I did

  • Prototyping and Construction
  • User Testing
  • Animation & Video editing

Skills used
