The How and Why factors behind Changi's passenger traffic growth

Data Visualisation

Passenger Traffic in Changi Airport

An interactive data visualisation involving multidimensional data on Changi Airport and its passenger traffic. Through interacting, viewers would be able to learn more about how much Changi’s passenger traffic has grown over the past twenty years and understand the factors contributing to this growth.

Key Words

Data Visualization, Information Graphics, Dynamic Communication

Target Audience

Adults or teens who are interested to learn more about Changi’s Passenger Traffic

Goal

To tell a narrative about Changi’s Passenger Traffic over the past twenty years through various data visualisations

Year of project

2020

 

What I did

  • Secondary research & Data collection
  • Data visualisation
  • Graphic Design
  • Front-end web development

Skills used

  • Coding: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Softwares: Adobe Illustrator, Tableau, Sublime Text 3
