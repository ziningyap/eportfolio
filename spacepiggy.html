An inanimate piggy bank comes to life

Space Piggy

when virtual meets reality

Space Piggy is a money-saving tool for young children that brings an inanimate piggy bank to life. Through storytelling and unpredictability, magic happens to Maggie the piggy bank when they drop a coin in. Every cent saved triggers a different reaction, getting children excited about saving money.

Key Words

Interaction Design, Storytelling, Money-saving

Target Audience

Children ages between 3 to 10

Goals

To encourage young children to save money by enhancing the immediate experience of saving money

Scope of project

To tell a story with the embodiment of Mixed Reality. Merging virtual and physical objects to co-exist and interact in real-time using the pepper's ghost effect.

Year of project

2020
